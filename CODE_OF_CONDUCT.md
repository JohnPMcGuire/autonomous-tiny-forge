# Community Conduct

Autonomous Tiny Forge welcomes thoughtful, curious, and constructive participation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Make room for beginners and different ways of communicating.
- Explain the impact of a problem instead of assuming intent.
- Respect privacy and do not post personal information, credentials, or private conversations.
- Keep contributions relevant to the project and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate disruption, impersonation, doxxing, spam, and repeated bad-faith behavior are not welcome.

## Project decisions

The repository owner may edit, hide, lock, or remove contributions that violate these guidelines or create a safety, privacy, legal, or security risk. Access may be removed when necessary.

## Reporting a concern

Do not place sensitive details in a public issue. Contact the repository owner privately through their GitHub profile and include only the information needed to understand the concern.

Participation in this project means agreeing to follow these guidelines in issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and other project spaces.
